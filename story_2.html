Amanda’s Tuition

When I was a kid, my Dad got really sick. I watched him suffer every day and felt really helpless when I couldn’t do anything. There was no one to take care of him. This inspired me to dedicate my life to helping the elderly in need. I attend a nursing school to get my degree and volunteer three days a week. But money is short, and working part-time while studying is not enough to pay for the last year of school.
